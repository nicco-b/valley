# Lore Bible

The source of truth for the world. Every quest, item description, dialogue
line, place name, and biome palette checks against what's written here.
Consistency is what makes a fictional place feel real — this is the canon.

Co-authored by both of us. Plain markdown, one file per topic, linked freely.
Entries here graduate into game records in `data/` as systems come online.

## Structure (grows as needed)

- `axioms.md` — the 2–3 founding premises everything else obeys. **Start here.**
- `phenomena.md` — the glow and other natural laws of this world *(create when axioms exist)*
- `places/` — regions, settlements (each answers: eat? trade? why here?)
- `people/` — characters, relationship webs
- `history.md` — the two legible eras
- `language.md` — naming rules, units, calendar

## Rules

1. Nothing is "magic" and nothing is "technology." Everything is this world's
   nature, named in its own terms.
2. Every entry must be a consequence of the axioms (or amend the axioms —
   deliberately).
3. Real-place test for settlements: what do they eat, what do they trade, why
   this spot?
4. Write it down or it isn't canon.
